## LeonardoAI

> https://leonardo.ai/

* Herramienta para generar imagenes que permite generar una cierta cantidad de imagenes de forma gratuita por dia
* Tip
  * En cualquier programa generador de imagenes se recomienda utilizar los prompts en ingles
* Permite
  * Texto a Imagen
    * Create a thrilling and dynamic scene of skydivers in mid-air above a breathtaking landscape during golden hour, capturing the excitement, freedom, and adrenaline of skydiving. Include a diverse group of people in colorful jumpsuits and helmets, with clear skies, soft clouds, and the earth far below, emphasizing motion and exhilaration, cinematic lighting, high detail, realistic style, no text.
  * Imagen a Imagen
    * Using the provided reference image of the person, create a high-energy, cinematic scene where they are the lead singer of a rock band performing in a massive stadium. Accurately preserve their facial features, skin tone, hairstyle, and overall likeness — it must clearly be the same person. Show them at the front of the stage holding a microphone, mid-performance, expressing intense passion and charisma. The stadium should be packed with a huge crowd going wild, hands in the air, dramatic stage lights, smoke effects, and vibrant concert lighting. Capture motion, energy, and atmosphere — dynamic composition, powerful stage presence, realistic details, epic scale, no text.
* Puntakje : 9 / 10

## Ideogram

> https://ideogram.ai/

Usamos este prompt
```
Create a thrilling and dynamic scene of skydivers in mid-air above a breathtaking landscape during golden hour, capturing the excitement, freedom, and adrenaline of skydiving. Include a diverse group of people in colorful jumpsuits and helmets, with clear skies, soft clouds, and the earth far below, emphasizing motion and exhilaration, cinematic lighting, high detail, realistic style. Add bold, clear Spanish text in the sky that reads: “Escuela de Paracaidismo”, professionally integrated into the composition, visually striking and easy to read.
```
* Puntaje : 9 / 10
